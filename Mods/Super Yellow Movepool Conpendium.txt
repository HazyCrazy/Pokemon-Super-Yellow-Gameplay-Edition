This exists to allow the Pokedex Data Page to serve as an in game wiki. 
There is flat out too many mechanics to keep track of to maintain 
an wiki with up to date information.

The only solution to this problem is having 8 to 10 Pokemon that learn every move in the game,
and the player can simply go to a Pokemon such as Ditto, to see what's going on with
the changes to the movepool for all Pokemon.
