Preconfigured Gen 4 Artstle Tileset are from Pokemon Vanguard,
that were based on a public tileset resource. 

During development, I needed a Gen 4 Artstle Tileset, 
where all 32 x 32 chunks were fully configured within RPG Maker XP.
During research, I found that Pokemon Vanguard was the only fangame,
that had Gen 4 artstyle tilesets, and the source project was included
with the download, allowing me to not waste development time configuring
every 32 x 32 chuck within a tileset .png. 

If it is an issue that the decision I made very early in development 
ended up being a poor decision, I will replace each chunk in the png tileset 
with HGSS tileset rips.
